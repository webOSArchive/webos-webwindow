Web Window - Help

Web Window is designed to be a simple app that keeps a single page open, refreshing periodically if configured.

By design, this app keeps your device on as long as the app is open and in-focus. This will drain your battery!

If you prefer, you can leave the app closed, and set it as your Exhibition app that launches when the screen turns off while the device is on the charger/Touchstone.

To get started, set your Home Page from the App Menu.
